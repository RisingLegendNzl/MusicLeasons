Getting Started with Beat Making

  • Understanding Tempo (BPM): Beats Per Minute dictate the speed of your track.
  • The Basic Drum Pattern: Kick on 1 and 3, Snare on 2 and 4, Hi-Hats on every eighth note is a common starting point.
  • Experiment with Sounds: Try different combinations and rhythms.
